About Seal Cafe

Blepi Seal Paradise is a one-of-a-kind concept that blends a pool resort and pet cafe in one! Families can come relax in our resort, as well as swim alongside our habilitated rescue seals. The resort also features a water-side cafe where visitors can enjoy fresh smoothies. We aim to provide an enriched experience for children through playful interaction with the seals.
